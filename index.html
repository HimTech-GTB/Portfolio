About Me

My name is Himal Bishwakarma, and I am a creative editor from Nepal with experience in videography, photography, poster design, and image editing. I enjoy transforming raw visuals into meaningful and eye-catching content that tells a clear story. For me, editing is not just about adding effects, but about improving quality, balance, and emotion` in every frame. I focus on details such as color correction, composition, lighting, and typography to make my work visually strong and professional. I enjoy creating posters and visual designs that communicate ideas clearly and attract attention at first glance. I always try to understand the purpose of a project before editing so the final result matches the vision and message. I am constantly learning new tools and techniques to improve my skills and keep up with modern design trends. My goal is to deliver creative, clean, and impactful visual content that leaves a lasting impression.
